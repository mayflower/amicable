# Quarkus Basics

## When To Use
- You are editing the Quarkus template.

## Conventions
- Keep JAX-RS resources focused on transport concerns.
- Move business logic into CDI-managed services.

## Preview
- The sandbox preview runs `./mvnw quarkus:dev` on port 3000.
